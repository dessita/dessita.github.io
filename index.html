About

I'm a UX/UI designer with more than 10 years of experience. I love the process of discovering the user experience in challenging situations through research, user testing and conversation and following best practices and UX patterns. Once the prototypes get approved and user tested I enjoy coding the visual and interactions working with developers to complete the conversion of the design into product. My programming skills help me design with consideration for the developer's hours that would need to be invested into building the product to ultimately deliver the most efficient result with the given resources.

I am originally from Bulgaria and currently living in South Florida. In my free time I love photography, playing volleyball, camping and playing Ukulele.
